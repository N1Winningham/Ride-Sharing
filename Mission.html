Our Mission

Our mission is to facilitate seamless transportation connections between student drivers and their peers on campus, harnessing the untapped potential of privately-owned vehicles. Recognized for addressing the unique challenges of locations underserved by traditional ride services and burdened by high rental costs from local monopolies, our platform offers a tailored solution for community-centric ride-sharing.
Experience fast, dependable, and superior ride services that guarantee door-to-door pick-ups and deliveries throughout Wooster and its surrounding areas. For students seeking to monetize their vehicles, our platform provides an excellent opportunity to transform your car into a valuable asset by matching you with fellow students in need of reliable transportation. Join us to redefine mobility on campus and contribute to a sustainable model of shared vehicle use.
